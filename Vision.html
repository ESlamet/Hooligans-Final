Who are we?

One Project, Many Artists.

One NFT, Endless Styles.

Hooligans seeks to bridge the gap between Web3 and Web2 Artists. Hooligans as a collective is an opportunity. An opportunity for all aspiring Web3 artists to embark on a new journey. A journey that promotes what NFTs are at its core, its artists and its community.

Hooligans aims to reach and connect with aspiring artists in the Web3 space. This project will have one NFT collection with many different artists and art styles. Each artist is allocated a base and a collection size, to which they are free to design as they please. Hooligans believe in REAL Utility. Not only are members of the community getting exclusive artist specific NFTs, but purchasing a Hooligans NFT also helps provide exposure and job opportunities for other prospective Web3 Artists. This opportunity helps to support the up-and-coming Web3 Artists but also promotes the uplfitment of the NFT space as whole.

By connecting with new artists eager to get into the space, Hooligans are increasing Web3 employment and involvement with artists from any background or following. And no, this isnt a one and done collection. Upon the first collections success, Hooligans will look to release another collection further employing more Artists in the space.
