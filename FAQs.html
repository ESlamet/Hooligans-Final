What are Hooligans?

Hooligans is a Solana NFT project that seeks provide jobs for up-and-coming artists looking to get a foothold in the Web3 space.

How does this benefit the NFT ecosystem?

Hooligans provides an opportunity to bridge the gap between Web2 and Web3 artists, but seeking artists from any following or background and offering them an opportunity to showcase their skills. This not only brings more people into the NFT space, but also helps promotes job opportunities and a general upliftment of the ecosystem.
